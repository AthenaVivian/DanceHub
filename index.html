Read-only Gmail source
Connect Gmail

      
One permission onlyhttps://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly
Tempo can search and read matching messages. It cannot send, delete, label, archive, or change Gmail settings.

      
Browser requirement
Google OAuth may not complete inside embedded browsers. Open Tempo in Chrome or Edge at http://localhost:4173, then connect Gmail there.

      
      
Dance email search
Tempo automatically searches supported booking signals and platform senders. This search is maintained by the app so older browser settings cannot omit classes.

      
History policy
Initial setup and Rebuild History scan the last 24 months. After that, Sync Now only reads new Gmail messages since the last successful sync. Existing history is kept cumulatively.
